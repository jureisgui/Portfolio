About me

I am originally from Brazil and have been living in New Zealand since February 2016. I came here for a career break and ended up staying because of the culture and beautiful sceneries.

Most of my adult life has been dedicated to teaching, and it was a career I fell in love with while studying something completely unrelated in university (Social Communications). I pursued my passion for teaching until the notorious burnout caught up with me after a decade. Since then, I found a new passion: coding. I enjoy the challenge and transforming ideas into tangible digital creations.

I have a good sense of humor and a lot of patience (probably because of my teaching background). I am quite adaptable too, having lived in three culturally distinct countries (USA, Finland, and now NZ) and worked across various industries like education, hospitality, customer service, and now IT.

I'm always up for new and exciting experiences! Some of my favorite adventure-filled memories include working at Disney World, deciding to go skydiving on a whim and taking the leap the same day, and braving the winter in Finland. But hey, not all adventures turn out to be a blast! One of my least favorite spur-of-the-moment decisions was bungee jumping, and let me tell you, I was absolutely petrified!
